What is R²?

Formula
R² measures how well the regression plane explains body length variation.

· R²=1.0 → perfect fit
· R²=0.0 → plane no better than the mean
· R²=0.85 → 85% of variation explained
The red dashed lines are residuals — vertical gap between each actual mouse 🟠 and the plane's prediction 🔵.

R² asks: are these gaps small relative to overall spread?
R² = 1 − SS_res / SS_tot

SS_res = Σ(yᵢ−ŷᵢ)² residual error
SS_tot = Σ(yᵢ−ȳ)² total variance
